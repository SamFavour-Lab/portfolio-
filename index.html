Featured Projects (AI Automation Case Studies)

🤖 AI Customer Support Ticket Automation

Problem: Companies receive large volumes of customer emails, making it difficult to manually categorize, prioritize, and respond quickly.

Solution: Built an AI-powered system that processes Gmail messages, analyzes them with an AI Agent, creates structured support tickets in Airtable, and sends instant responses.

Workflow: Gmail Trigger → Gmail Get a Message → AI Agent (Groq) → Structured Output Parser → Airtable (Create Ticket) → Conditional Logic → Gmail Response

Business Impact: Faster response time, reduced manual workload, improved customer satisfaction, scalable support system.

📈 AI Lead Qualification & CRM Automation

Problem: Businesses receive large volumes of inbound leads but struggle to qualify, prioritize, and route them efficiently.

Solution: An AI-powered system that analyzes incoming lead data, classifies intent, and automatically routes leads into CRM pipelines with notifications.

Workflow: Airtable Trigger → Groq AI (Create JSON Chat Completion for Lead Scoring) → JSON Parse → Router

  • Route A (Hot Leads): Airtable Update Record → Gmail Notification → Airtable Status Update
  • Route B (Warm Leads): Airtable Update Record → Gmail Notification → Airtable Follow-up Stage Update
  • Route C (Cold Leads): Airtable Update Record only (CRM tagging & segmentation)

Business Impact: Improved lead qualification accuracy, faster sales response time, and better CRM organization for sales teams.
